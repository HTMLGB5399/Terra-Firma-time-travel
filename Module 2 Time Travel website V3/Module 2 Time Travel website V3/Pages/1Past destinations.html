Terms of Travel

*Please note all travellers travelling back in time must have an awareness of the butterfly effect and complete a mandatory training that can take up to three weeks and incur extra costs. All participants must be aware that their actions in the past can affect those in the future so all efforts must be made to have limited contact with citizens, events and the surrounding environment. For more information, see our Terms of Travel.
